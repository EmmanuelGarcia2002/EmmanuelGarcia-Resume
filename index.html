I was born in Los Angeles, California in 2002. I have been in various parts of the LA area for a majority of my life. Currently, I live in El Monte, but have came to live off campus near UCR so I can meet new people and learn how to be an adult. I come from a Mexican background and currently a First Generation College Student here in UCR. I am the first person in my family to graduate from high school and make their way to higher education. In my free time, I enjoy reading fiction and better my body. My greatest strength, is my tenacity to never give up on what matters to me the most. My greatest weakness, would be my unwilliness to innovate and have a hard time adapting to new changes. As a First Generation College student, I have struggled to navigate and figure out on my own how the best recourse of action to earning my BA in History. Despite it all, I have no choice but to meet the expectations I placed on myself and hope to do better in the future.

My college Major is History. I have chosen History as my college major because I have a affinity to look at the past and learn from our ancestors. I am particularly interested in more modern history and how this envelopes the world we live in today. Currently, I am in my Fourth Year and anticipating to graduate soon. My intentions after graduating are to work at an NGO that is dedicated to helping the planet or for a government position which tells something about U.S. History. My future aims as a adult would be to be self-sufficient and to give something back to my community once I have built something for myself. My hopes in the future are to find a life partner and earn enough income to live and own a house.

The most important attributes I can bring into any job position would be my honesty and williness to learn. I hold myself accountable for my actions and do not wish to impose more than what is permitted of me. I might be inexperienced in the world, but I have the desire to try opportunities if I am given a chance. I have faced many setbacks on my path in life, but I am still trying to do better for myself and the people that matter to me. I hope to be a more sociable and kinder person than who I am now, and reconnect with the people that I have left in my life.
